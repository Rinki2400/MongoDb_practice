Benefits of NoSQL

1) Elastic Scaling
- Horizontal Scaling
- Handle large volume of data
- Load distribution
- Auto-sharding
- Reduced downtime
- Performance is improved during traffic hike

2) Avoid Joins
3) No need for a data to fit a schema
- Flexibility in Data modeling
- No data transformation overhead
- Good for Unstructured and Semi Structured data

4) Quick and easy development
- schema Flexibility
- Not much complexity
- Agile development and Rapid iteration

5) Ability to cope with Network failures
- highly available and reliable
- fault tolerant
- Built-in replication
- Partition tolerance (CAP)

6) Low latency and high Performance

7) Supports multiple data Structure

8) Less management
